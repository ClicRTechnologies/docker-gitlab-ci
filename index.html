Zero to GitLab CI in 5 seconds or less*

docker run --name=gitlab-ci -it --rm \
 -p 10080:80 -e 'GITLAB_URL=https://gitlab.com' \
 -v /var/run/docker.sock:/run/docker.sock \
 -v $(which docker):/bin/docker \
  sameersbn/gitlab-ci:latest

Point your browser to http://localhost:10080 and login using your gitlab.com credentials

* download and start up times not included
